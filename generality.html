Portfolio OVERVIEW

This portfolio is the main deliverable of the Innovative Smart Systems formation and will present all the works done this semester and the different skills acquired throughout all the courses. All those experiences will be presented in three ways :

  • A descriptive part that will enumerate all the experiences related to my training with their context and the functions I carried during them.
  • A technical part will present all the technical problems I encoutered throughout my training and how we solved them and thanks to this, demonstrate my skills and knowledge in the particular field.
  • An analytical part in which I will carry an exhaustive analysis of all the skills I managed to acquire during my training

And in this part, I will present my curriculum and all the courses I followed that are related to this training.
